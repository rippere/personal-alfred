# Portfolio Narrative

## Overview
Strategic job search and portfolio documentation repository containing application materials, target companies, career roadmap, and personal brand narrative.

## Purpose
Organize and execute strategic job search targeting roles in:
- Behavioral Science + AI companies
- AI Product/Strategy positions
- Fintech companies
- High-growth startups
- Research organizations

## Key Documents
- **JOB_SEARCH_README.md** - Quick start guide and overview
- **APPLICATION_CHECKLIST.md** - Application tracking and execution
- **APPLICATION_TEMPLATES.md** - 6 customizable templates by company type
- **COMPANY_TARGETS.md** - 21 target companies organized in 3 tiers
- **CAREER_ROADMAP.md** - Long-term career trajectory
- **PORTFOLIO_NARRATIVE.md** - Personal brand and story
- **INDEX.md** - Document navigation guide

## Target Companies (Tier 1 Focus)
- Irrational Labs (Behavioral Science + AI)
- Perplexity (AI Product)
- Receptiviti (Behavioral analytics)
- Additional rolling positions at strategic targets

## Application Strategy
Tier-based approach:
- **Tier 1**: High-priority rolling positions
- **Tier 2**: Seasonal hiring targets
- **Tier 3**: Backup and exploration targets

## Template Types
6 customizable templates for different company types:
1. Behavioral Science + AI firms
2. Pure AI/ML companies
3. Fintech companies
4. Startups (early stage)
5. Research organizations
6. Product/Strategy roles

## Current Status
Active job search documentation. Repository structure complete with comprehensive templates and target tracking.
