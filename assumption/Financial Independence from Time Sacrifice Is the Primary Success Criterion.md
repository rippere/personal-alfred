# Financial Independence from Time Sacrifice Is the Primary Success Criterion

## Claim

The explicit end goal is not prestige, title, or career achievement in the abstract — it is specifically freedom from sacrificing time to basic financial survival. "Becoming the person who no longer has to sacrifice time to put food on the table."

## Basis

Directly stated in May 2025 journal as the motivating end state behind niche-depth hireability work. Financial independence through specialized expertise is the terminal goal; hireability and niche depth are instrumental paths to it, not ends in themselves.

## Evidence Trail

- May 2025 journal: goal framed as "becoming the person who no longer has to sacrifice time to put food on the table — financial independence through specialized expertise"

## Impact

Distinguishes Ben's motivation from prestige-seeking or credential-chasing. Decisions and career moves should be evaluated against this terminal criterion, not intermediate proxies like job titles or institutional affiliation. The niche-depth model is the how; this is the why.
