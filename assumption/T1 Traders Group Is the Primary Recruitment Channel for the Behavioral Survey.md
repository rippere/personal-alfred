# T1 Traders Group Is the Primary Recruitment Channel for the Behavioral Survey

## Claim
The behavioral survey is designed to recruit from the T1 traders group specifically, not the general retail trading population. This group is assumed to be a sufficiently representative and accessible sample of retail memecoin/crypto traders for the research goals.

## Basis
Note 1 explicitly specifies: "targeting traders (specifically T1 traders group)" as the survey population. Note 2 uses looser language ("retail traders") for the same study, suggesting T1 is the specific recruitment channel rather than a formal analytical definition.

## Evidence Trail
- 2025-10-15/16: Both research notes describe the same survey with differing specificity about the recruitment channel. No further confirmation or challenge found.

## Impact
Sample composition and generalizability depend on who constitutes the T1 traders group. If T1 is a specific online community rather than a formal tier, conclusions may not generalize to all retail crypto traders and research findings should be scoped accordingly.
