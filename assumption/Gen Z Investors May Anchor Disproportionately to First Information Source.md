# Gen Z Investors May Anchor Disproportionately to First Information Source

## Claim
Gen Z retail investors are more susceptible to anchoring effects from their first information source — particularly short-form content platforms like TikTok or news headlines — when making investment decisions compared to other investor cohorts.

## Basis
Proposed as a research hypothesis in the Oct 2025 behavioral finance brainstorming session. Framed as: "Does first information source (TikTok, news headlines) disproportionately anchor Gen Z investment decisions?"

## Evidence Trail
- 2025-10-15: Hypothesis generated in research brainstorming session alongside Memecoin Sentiment Analyzer design. No empirical data collected yet.

## Impact
If confirmed, has implications for the Memecoin Sentiment Analyzer (Gen Z is likely over-represented in memecoin retail trading) and as a standalone study connected to WSU Psychology Coursework.
