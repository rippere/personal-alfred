# Shelve Memecoin and Sector Flow Projects in Favor of EMM

## Context

The Memecoin Sentiment Analyzer and Sector Flow projects were active educational/exploratory data science projects. A portfolio review determined that the Executive Mind Matrix (EMM) was a higher-priority initiative warranting full focus.

## Options Considered

1. **Continue both projects in parallel** — maintain development momentum on Memecoin and Sector Flow alongside EMM
2. **Shelve both in favor of EMM** — deprioritize the two data science projects to concentrate effort on EMM

## Decision

Shelve Memecoin Sentiment Analyzer and Sector Flow projects. Redirect attention to Executive Mind Matrix as the primary initiative.

## Rationale

EMM represents a higher-value initiative. Both Memecoin and Sector Flow had achieved their educational goals (the architecture, tooling, and learning objectives were substantially complete) and did not require active development to preserve their value.

## Consequences

- Memecoin Sentiment Analyzer paused: planned Observability Stack integration and potential nw_wrld VJ Platform data visualization integration were not pursued
- EMM received full development attention
- Both shelved projects remain available for revival if prioritization changes
