# Memecoin Sentiment Analyzer Originated from Broader Social Network Analysis Vision

## Insight
The Memecoin Sentiment Analyzer project emerged from a broader Oct 2025 research session that conceived multiple related projects simultaneously. The Twitter/social epicenter scan — analyzing community hotspot clusters to identify memecoin liquidity concentration before price action — was explicitly identified as a "direct precursor" to the Memecoin Sentiment Analyzer. This means the project's conceptual foundation is social network graph analysis, with the behavioral survey design added as a complementary measurement layer, not the other way around.

## Evidence
Both source notes describe the epicenter scan idea from the same Oct 15–16 brainstorming session that produced the behavioral survey design. Note 2 explicitly labels the epicenter scan as a "Direct precursor to the Memecoin Sentiment Analyzer project." The same session also generated the Gen Z anchoring study idea, suggesting a broader behavioral finance research agenda that MSA was part of.

## Implications
The social network analysis component is not a later addition — it is the original design intent. If the Twitter/epicenter component is ever de-scoped, it removes the project's foundational concept. Survey-only analysis would represent a significant reduction from the original vision. The project was conceived as a multi-layer system: network signal → behavioral survey → market response.

## Applicability
Relevant to scope decisions, resource allocation between the behavioral survey and network analysis components, and any decision to prioritize one component over the other. Also relevant to understanding why the Twitter virality extension is structurally central rather than optional.
